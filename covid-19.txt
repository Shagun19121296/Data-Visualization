COVID-19 Data Analysis
Description: Analyze COVID-19 case data for various countries and regions. You can perform time-series analysis to understand trends and compare the impact in different regions.
Key Tasks:
Clean and preprocess COVID-19 data (e.g., case counts, deaths, recoveries).
Calculate total cases and growth rates.
Use Pandas to group and aggregate data.
Visualize the growth trend using Matplotlib or Seaborn.
